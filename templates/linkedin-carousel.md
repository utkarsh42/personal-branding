## Design Notes

### Color Scheme
- **Background**: [Primary color - e.g., Deep blue]
- **Text**: [White or contrasting color]
- **Accents**: [Secondary color for highlights]
- **Brand**: [Include subtle branding]

### Typography
- **Heading font**: Bold, easy to read (e.g., Inter, Helvetica)
- **Body font**: Clean, readable (e.g., Inter, Roboto)
- **Size**: Large enough to read on mobile

### Layout
- **Margins**: Generous spacing
- **Hierarchy**: Clear heading vs body
- **Icons/Visuals**: Minimal, purposeful
- **Consistency**: Same template across slides

### Tools
- **Canva**: Easy carousel templates
- **Figma**: Custom design control
- **Adobe Express**: Quick creation

---

## Carousel Length Guide

- **Quick tip**: 5-6 slides (one main insight)
- **Standard**: 8-10 slides (3-5 insights + intro/outro)
- **Comprehensive**: 10-12 slides (deep-dive topic)

**Note**: LinkedIn allows up to 20 slides, but engagement drops after slide 10

---

## Pre-Publish Checklist

- [ ] Slide 1 hook is compelling
- [ ] Each slide has one clear point
- [ ] Text is readable on mobile
- [ ] Specific metrics and examples included
- [ ] Visual consistency across slides
- [ ] Clear CTA in final slide
- [ ] Caption complements carousel (doesn't repeat it)
- [ ] Hashtags relevant (3-5 max)
- [ ] Aligns with content pillar
- [ ] Target audience will find valuable
- [ ] Spelling and grammar checked
- [ ] Designed in 1080x1080 or 1080x1350 format
