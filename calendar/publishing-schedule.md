# Publishing Schedule

## Weekly Cadence

### LinkedIn
**Frequency**: 3-4 posts/week

**Days**:
- **Monday**: Technical or Strategy post
- **Wednesday**: Case study or Deep-dive
- **Friday**: Building in Public weekly update (consistent)
- **Sunday** (optional): Quick insight or milestone

**Best Times** (IST):
- **Morning**: 9-11 AM (office start, checking feeds)
- **Lunch**: 1-2 PM (lunch break scroll)
- **Evening**: 5-7 PM (commute, end-of-day scroll)
- **Best overall**: 6 PM IST on weekdays

**Post Length**: 1,000-1,500 words ideal

---

### Twitter/X
**Frequency**: Daily (1-2 posts/day)

**Content Types**:
- **Monday**: Thread (8-10 tweets) - Major insight or tutorial
- **Tuesday-Thursday**: Daily insights (1-2 tweets)
- **Friday**: Weekly wrap-up (3-5 tweet thread)
- **Weekend**: Light activity or replies only

**Best Times** (IST):
- **Morning**: 8-10 AM (coffee scroll)
- **Lunch**: 1-3 PM (lunch break)
- **Evening**: 6-8 PM (commute, evening scroll)
- **Best overall**: 8 AM IST for threads, 6 PM for quick tweets

**Thread Length**: 8-10 tweets (standard), up to 15 for deep-dives

---

### Blog/Substack
**Frequency**: 2x/month (bi-weekly)

**Days**:
- **2nd Wednesday** of month: Technical tutorial or deep-dive
- **4th Wednesday** of month: Monthly retrospective or case study

**Length**: 2,000-3,000 words with code examples, diagrams

**Repurpose**: Blog posts → LinkedIn summaries → Twitter threads

## Platform-Specific Strategy

### LinkedIn

**Post Structure**:
1. Hook (stop scrolling)
2. Story/Context (2-3 paragraphs)
3. Key insights (3-5 bullets with specifics)
4. CTA (question or invitation)
5. Hashtags (3-5 relevant)

**Engagement**:
- Respond to all comments within 2 hours of posting
- Continue engaging for 24-48 hours
- Share insights from comments in future posts

**Hashtags** (rotate based on topic):
- Core: #AIProducts, #ProductManagement, #BuildInPublic
- Technical: #LangChain, #RAG, #n8n
- GTM: #GTMStrategy, #ProductLedGrowth, #MarketingAutomation
- Domain: #IndiaStartups, #StartupIntelligence

---

### Twitter/X

**Thread Structure**:
1. Hook tweet (1/10)
2. Context (2/10)
3. Main points (3-8/10)
4. Key takeaway (9/10)
5. Summary + CTA (10/10)

**Engagement**:
- Respond to replies within 1 hour
- Quote tweet interesting reactions
- Bookmark and reshare best replies

**Hashtags**:
- Minimal (1-2 max) or none
- Only use if trending and relevant

---

### Blog

**Post Structure**:
1. Introduction (problem/context)
2. Main content (3-5 sections with code/examples)
3. Results/Metrics
4. Key takeaways
5. Next steps/Resources

**Distribution**:
- Publish on blog
- Share summary on LinkedIn (same day)
- Break into Twitter thread (next day)
- Email to newsletter subscribers (if/when you have one)

## Content Batch Creation Workflow

### Step 1: Ideation (30 mins)
- Review content-ideas.md
- Check what resonated last week
- Pick 3-4 topics for the week
- Align with content pillars

### Step 2: Drafting (2 hours)
- Use templates from templates/
- Write LinkedIn posts for Mon/Wed
- Draft Twitter thread for Monday
- Outline Friday Building in Public

### Step 3: Review (30 mins)
- Check positioning alignment
- Verify metrics/specifics are accurate
- Run through quality checklist
- Schedule or save drafts

## Repurposing Strategy

### Blog Post → Multiple Formats
1. **Blog**: Full 2,500-word tutorial (Wednesday)
2. **LinkedIn**: 1,500-word summary (same day)
3. **Twitter Thread**: Key insights breakdown (next day)
4. **LinkedIn Carousel**: Visual summary (following week)

### Building in Public → Multiple Platforms
1. **LinkedIn**: Full weekly update (Friday 6 PM)
2. **Twitter Thread**: Highlights + learnings (Friday 7 PM)
3. **Newsletter**: Monthly compilation (if/when)

### Case Study → Multiple Angles
1. **LinkedIn**: Full case study (Week 1)
2. **Twitter Thread**: Technical breakdown (Week 2)
3. **Blog**: Deep-dive tutorial (Week 3)
4. **Carousel**: Visual framework (Week 4)

## Emergency Content

- Interesting metric or finding
- Reframe a comment into a post

**15-Minute Posts**:
- "3 Things I Learned This Week"

- Quick thread on recent learning

**Evergreen Backup**:
- "Ask Me Anything" post

## Quality Gates

Before publishing:
- [ ] Aligns with content pillar
- [ ] Reinforces positioning
- [ ] Includes specific metrics or examples
- [ ] Target audience will find valuable
- [ ] Grammar and formatting clean
- [ ] CTA encourages engagement
- [ ] Hashtags relevant
- [ ] Scheduled for optimal time
